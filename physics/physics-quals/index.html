UC San Diego Physics PhD qualifying exams from 1987 through 2019.

UCSD Physics Qualifying Exams Archive

I’ve maintained the UCSD Physics Qualifying Exams Archive on my website since 2008! This page provides access to a comprehensive collection of past qualifying exams for physics graduate students. Note: UCSD stopped administering qualifying exams in 2019, so there are no newer exams. If you have an exam that I’m missing or a correction please let me know!
